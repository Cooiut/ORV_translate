"Let's take the stairs."

At Jung Heewon's words, we turned toward the emergency staircase. Since many of our group were elderly, we wanted to avoid the stairs at all costs, but we had no choice.

"Don't worry! My body is hardened by walking 10,000 steps every day!"

Poseidon, adjusting his Marine Corps cap, shouted boldly as he walked past me. Indeed, does the God of the Sea climb stairs well? I don't know where on earth he walked 10,000 steps while being trapped in an abandoned factory all this time, but—following the constellations letting out thunderous shouts, I climbed the stairs feeling like the youngest member of a hiking club. Honestly, climbing stairs was torture for me, too. And 50 floors to begin with. With a body that couldn't even use the system, it was a height no different from Mount Everest.

「Kim Dokja thought.」

He simply couldn't hold back his rising breath without writing it down.

Jung Heewon, who had been running at the front, shouted as she looked down at the landing. Her entire body was covered in someone's blood, suggesting the cleanup upstairs was already finished. She grabbed my wrist and pulled me along, glancing at the constellations behind her.

"Leave them behind. You said there was no time."

It seemed Jung Heewon hadn't placed much hope in the constellations to begin with. However, I couldn't simply cast aside even a small part of my strength.

"Heewon-ssi, over here!"

The emergency exit on the stairwell opened, and the men in suits who had been waiting rushed in. As Jung Heewon swung her wooden sword to push them back, screams began to be heard from the floor below. The constellations, caught by the tail, were being brutally beaten by the men in suits.

Finally, unable to stand it any longer, Poseidon stepped forward.

「Poseidon let out the breath he had been holding.」

Toward the men charging in with a human wave, Poseidon began spitting water from his mouth.

"Ooooooh!"

The men in suits, suddenly struck by a water cannon mixed with Poseidon's saliva, recoiled in anger. The cannon soon turned into a weak stream of water.

"Cough, darling, just a glass of water—"

Demeter, holding a water bottle nearby, quickly gave Poseidon some water.

"Hit him now!"

While Poseidon hesitated, the men in suits had come right up to his face. Poseidon, whose water cannon hadn't finished recharging yet, stood awkwardly, clutching a broken recorder.

It was Zeus, who had been watching all this time, who pushed Poseidon aside and stepped forward.

Tsutsutsutsutsu!

The sound of an electric shocker striking the floor. The men, electrocuted in succession, recoiled in shock and fainted. However, only the men in the front row had collapsed. The lower floor was still packed with men in suits chasing us. Constellations and men were engaged in a chaotic battle all around.

"Leave us behind. It will be troublesome if we get delayed."

Demeter spoke to me, looking at me. It was the first time she had spoken in such a clear voice, so I listened to her with a slight surprise.

"I don't know what is going on, but you are likely the only one here who can stop 'him'."

Demeter spoke as if she knew who was waiting for me on this rooftop.

"I will chase after them soon. I have no intention of yielding this myth to you."

Zeus added, growling as well. Judging by the situation, it seemed I should leave this place to them and head up.

I exchanged glances with Dansu ahjussi and Jung Heewon, then immediately went upstairs.

Before I knew it, the floor count had passed the 20th and then the 30th. However, the situation was not going smoothly. Just as we reached the 35th floor, we came face-to-face with a massive wall in the middle of the landing.

Upon closer inspection, it wasn't a wall, but a human back.

"Hyunsung-ssi?"

Lee Hyunsung, his eyes strangely clouded, turned to look at me.

"Dokja-ssi... I'm sorry."

"Yes?"

Just as I thought something was off, Jung Heewon pushed me aside and ran forward. At the same time, Lee Hyunsung, who had rushed forward, collided with Jung Heewon in the middle. Lee Hyunsung's fist and Jung Heewon's wooden sword clashed, and the broken tip of the sword tumbled across the floor.

"Heewon-ssi! Hyunsung-ssi!"

"This person is unconscious!"

"Dokja-ssi, I'm sorry..."

Lee Hyunsung, who was merely repeating the same words. He was currently in a state where normal 'record execution' was impossible.

Every time Lee Hyunsung's fist and Jung Heewon's baton clashed, a voice called out to me.

"Because I wanted to fight this person at least once."

Before that, I had to reach the rooftop of the building and stop 'Snowfield Kim Dokja'.

44th floor, 45th floor… We continued running upwards. Dansu ahjussi gasped for breath but never once showed a hint of fatigue. Just as we reached the 46th floor, I glanced back at him and asked.

"Ahjussi."

In the truck, Dansu ahjussi apologized to me.

Dansu ahjussi shook his head coolly and gazed at the marker indicating the ascent to the 47th floor.

There must be a reason why he is being honest with me. Perhaps he is in a state where it is difficult for him to take a definite side, either me or Snowfield Kim Dokja.

His daughter—Jiyoon's soul is in the theater of 'Asmodeus'. If so, perhaps he has decided to get Jiyoon back in exchange for delivering the 'Book' to the third child.

We looked out the window. Just as the sunlight seemed to vanish in an instant, a massive shadow enveloped the building. Screams could be heard from somewhere. The sensation of the unreality becoming reality. The downy hairs on my body stood on end.

The Beast Lord's partner.

"Go! Hurry!"

I shook my head. Even for you, it is impossible. With your current body, you cannot face a monster like that.

"Have you forgotten who I am?"

Dazzling stories were shimmering across his entire body.

The golden robes that enveloped the Great Sage Equal to Heaven's entire body, Thunder flashed from the clouds enveloping his feet.

The 49th floor.

A sign reading 'No Entry for Unauthorized Personnel' was posted in front of the door leading to the rooftop. It seemed as though the sign was speaking. That from here on, only those involved in this story are allowed to enter.

As if asking, "How dare someone like you call yourself an 'authorized person'?"

「Who could possibly speak of this story?」

"You're late."
